Build real simulations, write impactful code, and turn ideas into working models—while living in **Shenzhen**, one of China’s most vibrant innovation hubs.  The **Computational Particle Methods Laboratory** at **Shenzhen University** warmly welcomes motivated, hands-on students and researchers.

### Research Focus 
- Intricate granular media behavior (from micro-mechanics to emergent macroscopic response)
- Offshore geotechnics (seabed response, foundations, and marine geohazards)
- Fluid–soil interaction (coupled multiphase processes, seepage, erosion, liquefaction-related phenomena)

### Methods & Tools
- LBM (Lattice Boltzmann Method)
- MPM (Material Point Method)
- DEM (Discrete Element Method)
